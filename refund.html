Subscription Plans

SpeechyVoice offers various subscription plans to its users. By subscribing to any of our plans, you agree to pay the fees associated with the plan you have chosen, and you acknowledge that you understand our Refund Policy.

Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription to the Service at any time. To cancel your subscription, you can click on the "Manage Billing" button on your account page, which will redirect you to the Paddle billing management page. Alternatively, you can contact us at support@speechyvoice.com for assistance with the cancellation process. Your cancellation will take effect at the end of the current billing cycle, and you will not be charged again unless you choose to reactivate your subscription.

No Refunds

All subscription fees and payments for additional characters are non-refundable. When you cancel your subscription or discontinue the use of additional characters, you will not receive a refund for any fees already paid. Instead, you will continue to have access to the Service and the additional characters, if applicable, for the remainder of the current billing cycle.

If you have any issues with our Service or the additional characters, please contact us at support@speechyvoice.com, and we will do our best to resolve the problem.

Price Changes

We reserve the right to change the fees for our subscription plans at any time. We will notify you in advance of any price changes, and you will have the option to continue with your subscription at the new price or cancel your subscription.
