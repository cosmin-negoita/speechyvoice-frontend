Cookies

Cookies are files with a small amount of data, which may include an anonymous unique identifier. Cookies are sent to your browser from a web site and stored on your computer's hard drive.

We use "cookies" to collect information and enhance your experience on our Service. You have the option to "Accept" or "Reject" cookies when using our Service. You can also instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. If you choose to reject cookies, you will still be able to use our Service, but some features may be limited or unavailable.
