Frequently asked questions

How long does the free trial last?

Your free trial credits you with 10,000 characters, which happens to be around 12minutes of audio generation, on average. Once you run out, you can purchase additional characters on the free trial, but they will be at a much higher price compared to our subscribers (look at pricing page for further details)

How is my payment processed?

All payments will be processed by Paddle. Paddle is a payment provider that allows businesses to accept payments online. They are a UK-based company that is authorized and regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA) as an Electronic Money Institution (EMI) under the Electronic Money Regulations 2011.

How exactly do I use up my characters?

Whenever you press the 'play' button in a text block and we have to use up computational resources, we will deduct the appropriate number of characters from your allowance. Once you've generated the audio, it is saved on our servers and we won't charge you if you want to listen to the audio again, it's only when you change the contents of the text box that the audio will be removed (use the lock feature to stop yourself accidentally changing the blocks content)
